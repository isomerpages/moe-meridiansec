<h3>ABOUT US</h3>
<p>Meridian basketball players are committed to excellence in the sport where
the values of</p>
<p>discipline, respect, resilience, and team spirit are demonstrated in all
our players. While</p>
<p>mastery of basketball skills and physical fitness are important, we strongly
believe that it is even</p>
<p>more important that our boys have the correct values and character. We
place great emphasis</p>
<p>on inculcating discipline and sport values, like tenacity, resilience
and teamwork.</p>

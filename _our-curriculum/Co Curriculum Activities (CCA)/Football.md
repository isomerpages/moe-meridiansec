<h3></h3>
<h2>About Us</h2>
<p></p>
<p>Founded in 2017, Meridian Football has rapidly grown into a powerhouse
under the expert leadership of Coach Fabio. In a respectful and supportive
environment, our footballers are nurtured to reach their full potential,
both on and off the field.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Over the years, our B and C Division teams have consistently ranked among
the top 4 in their respective divisions, showcasing their dedication, discipline,
and passion for the game.</p>
<p></p>

<p>&nbsp;With every season, we continue to build on our legacy, striving
for excellence and fostering a love for football in every player.</p>

in Florida, home to the renowned football programme that has produced many

<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">

</li>
<li>
<p>National School Games ‘B’ Girls Champion</p>

<p>National Schools Games ‘C’ Girls Champion</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>National Schools Games ‘C’ Boys Finalist</p>
</li>

<p>2024 marked a historic year for Meridian Football as all four of our teams
reached the finals of the National School Games (NSG). Our B Division boys,
B Division girls, and C Division girls clinched the coveted Championship
titles, solidifying our reputation as a formidable force in Secondary School
Football.</p>

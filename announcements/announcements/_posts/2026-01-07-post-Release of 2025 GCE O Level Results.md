<p>Parents/Guardians &amp; Secondary 4E, 4N, 5N and 3E students who sat for
the O-Level Examination in 2025</p>
<p>1. The O-Level Examination results will be released on&nbsp;<strong>Wednesday, 14 January 2026.</strong>
</p>
<p>2. Students will receive their results from 2.00 pm onwards in the School
Hall. Students and accompanying parents are to be seated in the hall by
1.45 pm.</p>
<p>3. Students are to be in <strong>proper school attire and personal grooming</strong> to
receive their results. Students who are not in proper school attire and
personal grooming, separate arrangements will be made for the collection
of their results.&nbsp;&nbsp; They may be directed to a different venue
and collection timing may be affected.</p>
<p>4. Students who are unwell should seek medical attention and not return
to school to collect their results.</p>
<p>5. Students who are unable to collect their results in person may contact
their Form Teacher to arrange for a proxy to collect a physical copy of
their results from the school. Proxies are required to produce the relevant
documents for the schoolâ€™s verification when collecting the results. You
may contact the school for assistance with the appointment of a proxy.</p>
<p>6. Parents/Guardians are invited to be present during the release of results.</p>
